DeadHash is an utility to calculate <i>hashes</i>.

<b>Features:</b>
<ul>
    <li>Generate hashes for files and texts </li>
    <li>Dark theme support</li>
    <li>Compare your hases</li>
    <li>Supports multiple hashes</li>
</ul>

Supported hashes:
* MD5
* SHA-1
* SHA-224
* SHA-256
* SHA-384
* SHA-512
* CRC32
